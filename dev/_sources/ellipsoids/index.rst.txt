.. _ellipsoids:

Available Ellipsoids
====================

These are the available ellipsoids and their corresponding defining parameters.

All ellipsoids are instances of the :class:`~boule.Ellipsoid` and
:class:`~boule.Sphere` classes. See the class documentations for a list their
derived physical properties (attributes) and computations/transformations that
they can perform (methods).
